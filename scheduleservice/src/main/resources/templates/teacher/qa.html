Q&A

On this page, you can find answers to the questions we get the most. When using the system as a teacher, we suggest that you create your lecture plan in excel or similair and then upload a CSV file.

Courses

You can only see the courses your assigned too, if you cannot see the course you wish to edit, please contact an Course administrator.

Lectures

A Lecture has a start date/time and an end date/time. The location of the lecture and teachers (available users from students and teachers that are assigned to the lecture). If your indoubt about any of the fields, your not forced to fill them out.

Lecture Subject

After you created your lecture, you can assign lecture subjects to it. Lecture subjects are like headlines for the lecture, like
Class today: Where you could write about what you wanna talk about.
Homework: Homework for the students.

When you create a lecture subject, you assign it a priority, default is 50, lecture subjects are sorted by priority in descending order.

Lecture Item

Lecture items are assigned under Lecture Subjects, it could be that you had a Lecture Subject called Homework and you wanted to add a Lecture Item which said "Read page 1-50 in Forman".

Upload schedule

When uploading a schedule, all lectures in the course are deleted.
The file you upload, must be in UTF8 semicolon seperated CSV format
The format of the schedule must be

Headerline: Dato;Start;Slut;Teacher(s);Room;Subject1;Subject2 etc.

When a lecture start, the row must start with Dato and Start Slut, Teacher(s) and Room.
After the initial lecture start line, you can keep writting teachers and subject items.
